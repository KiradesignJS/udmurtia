interesting facts

Eggs
The Udmurt poultry farm Varaksino supplied eggs to the entire McDonald's network in Russia. In just eight months of 2019, the poultry farm delivered 3,800,000 eggs.
Mistery
One of the most mysterious zones of Udmurtia is K-16. It is located near the village of Krasnogorskoe. People saw here luminous balls. And after 40 minutes the head starts to spin and knocks at the temples.

Music
The Udmurt city of Votkinsk is the birthplace of the great composer Pyotr Tchaikovsky.
Tree
One of the oldest pines was found in Udmurtia, it is about 360 years old. The pine was the sacred tree of Inmar, the supreme deity of the Udmurts.
Motorcycle race
In 1936, the first motorcycle race for women in Russia was held in Udmurtia. Participants covered more than 1000 km off-road.
